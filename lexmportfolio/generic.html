Let's Connect!

Hi, I’m Lex Martinez!

I’m a senior graphic design student at San Jose State University with an associates in studio art from Berkeley City College. My journey into graphic design began with an intro class that sparked my passion, and I’ve been dedicated to honing my skills ever since.

I aspire to become an impactful designer, creating work that drives social change and promotes environmental stewardship. My ultimate dream is to contribute my creativity to a museum, where I can help preserve and share stories that inspire others.

As I continue to grow and expand my portfolio, I’m excited about opportunities to collaborate and make a meaningful difference through design. Let’s create something extraordinary together!
